THE MAZE GAME

• Complete over 20 levels!
• Get to the finish line.
• Avoid and kill monsters

ABOUT THE GAME

The Maze Game is a game where you have to complete 20 levels to win. You'll have to find your way through the maze, and
during your path, collect diamonds to unlock doors, collect coins to gain extra points, and avoid (and kill) monsters before they destory you.

INSTRUCTIONS AND TIPS


CHARACTER

This is your character. You will have to lead him through the maze.



FLAG

This flag is the finish line. Once you've reached it, you can then move on to the next level.



DOOR

This is the door. The door is mainly placed in front of the goal.
The only way it can be unlocked is if you collect all the diamonds.



DIAMOND

This is the diamond. These diamonds are placed all over the maze and when collected,
the door (shown above) will unlock, and you will finish the level.



MONSTER #1

The game gets a little more tricky. Monsters like this one are placed throughout the game. They move left and right only.
You need to avoid them to get to the flag by waiting for them to pass.If they hit you, the level restarts.



MONSTER #2

This is the second monster in the game. This monster moves up and down only. You need to avoid it to stay alive.



MONSTER #3

This is the third monster. Unlike the others, this monster moves around in all directions.
Once you're in contact with the ghost, you lose a life.



RING

This ring is placed in levels where there are Monster #3's. Once you collect this ring,
all these ghosts turn invisible and see-through. Meaning, you will be able to pass through the ghosts without
losing a life/without them hurting you. But, it is only for a limited amount of time.



INVISIBLE GHOSTS

This is what happens to Monster #3 (also know as ghost) when you collect the ring. Like explained
above, they turn see-through so you will be able to pass through them.



HOLE

Above is a picture of a hole. When you see a hole in the game, you cannot avoid it since it's
empty and you will fall inside. Look below for solution. (Hole is originally black in the game)



ROCK

This is a rock. It is used for two reasons. The first is to fill in holes (look above for
more information) so they can be able to walk over, and the second is to trap monsters.



BONUS COINS

These coins are distributed around the maze. They are there for you to collect if you want
extra points or if you want a higher score.



HEART

This is a heart. If you are running out of lives, you can collect this heart and you will
gain an extra life.
