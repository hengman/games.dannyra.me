INFORMATION



The game contains 20 levels. In each of those levels, you need to survive and find
your way through the maze. You have 3 lives, but you can gain more if you collect the hearts. If you
lose all your lives, the game will restart and you'll have to play
again from the beginning. To make your score higher, you can collect the coins.
Once you've completed the game, your highscore will pop up and you will have
to write your name next to the number.
